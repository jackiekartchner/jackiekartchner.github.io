Who I am

Little did I know that my obsession with maps and world cultures would lead me into my career path - first as a geography student and then into web development.

Geography studies is cartography and computer science as well as world affairs, current events, and geopolitical issues. I took Geographic Information System (GIS) classes and was able to quickly grasp GIS concepts and help other students in my class with several projects. This led to a GIS teaching assistant job where I guided students in using real-life satellite and drone imagery and turning the data into readable, aesthetic, and useful maps using ESRI and ENVI software.

Recently graduated from Actualize Coding Bootcamp learning web development with Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, Vue.js, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and SQL. I'm looking into web-mapping careers that can combine both GIS and web development.
